Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

At PULLEDOUT.LOL ("we", "us", or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Advanced Web Security Analysis Platform ("Service").

📌 Quick Summary

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the Service. We do not sell your information. You control your data and can request deletion at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Username: Your chosen username for account identification
  • Password: Stored as a cryptographically hashed value (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Email Address: Optional, if provided for account recovery or notifications
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of when your account was created
  • Last Login Date: Timestamp of your most recent login

1.2 Scan Data

When you use our security scanning features, we collect and store:

  • Target URLs: Website addresses and domains you submit for scanning
  • Scan Results: Vulnerability findings, security issues, and analysis data
  • Scan Metadata: Date, time, scan settings, and scan duration
  • Generated Reports: HTML and data reports generated from scans
  • Scan History: Record of all scans performed under your account

1.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical data when you use the Service:

  • IP Address: Your internet protocol address for security and fraud prevention
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and language preferences
  • Device Information: Operating system, device type, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, timestamps, and interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: Load times, errors, and service performance metrics
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preference storage

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real names or personal identification (unless you include it in your username)
  • Payment information (service is currently free)
  • Sensitive personal data (health, financial, or biometric data)
  • Social media profiles or third-party account information
  • Location data beyond IP-based geolocation for security

We use collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Operation

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Process and store security scan requests
  • Generate and deliver scan reports
  • Maintain scan history and user preferences
  • Enable core features and functionality

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Identify and fix bugs, errors, and performance issues
  • Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Optimize scanning algorithms and detection capabilities
  • Conduct internal analytics and research

2.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access or account abuse
  • Monitor for malicious activity or Terms of Service violations
  • Protect against fraud, spam, and security threats
  • Enforce rate limits and prevent service abuse
  • Investigate and respond to security incidents

2.4 Communication

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important service announcements and updates
  • Notify you of changes to Terms of Service or Privacy Policy

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit

  • Database Security: Encrypted database connections and access controls
  • Authentication: Session-based authentication with secure tokens
  • Access Controls: Role-based access and principle of least privilege

4.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
